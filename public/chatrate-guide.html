Getting started

Share one link. Clients book, pay, and join — no back-and-forth scheduling.

Flat rate or per-minute

Optional transcripts

Setup

Onboarding — Step by Step

The full setup takes about 5 minutes. Here's what each step asks for.

1

Choose your username

This becomes the last part of your booking URL. Use your name or brand — keep it short and memorable. Only lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens.
Example: chatrate-app.com/book/janesmith

2

Build your profile

Add your display name, a short bio (2–3 sentences about what you offer), and select your primary service type from the dropdown. This is what clients see on your booking page.

3

Set your rates

Choose between two pricing models:

Model How it works Best for
Per Minute Client pays for exact time used. Live ticker during call. Open-ended consultations
Flat Rate Fixed price per call, regardless of length. Fixed-length sessions

You can also set an optional transcript add-on fee — clients pay extra to receive a full written transcript after the call. Set to $0 to disable.

4

Connect your Stripe account

This is where call payments will land. You'll need two keys from your Stripe dashboard. See the next section for detailed instructions.

✅
After onboarding You'll pay the one-time $4.99 activation fee via Stripe. Once that clears, your page goes live instantly and you'll see your booking link.
Payments

Getting Your Stripe Keys

ChatRate uses your own Stripe account to process call payments. This means money goes directly to your bank — we never hold or touch it.

Don't have a Stripe account?

1

Go to stripe.com

Click "Start now" and create a free account with your email.

2

Complete identity verification

Stripe requires your name, address, and last 4 digits of SSN (or business EIN) to activate live payments. This takes 2–5 minutes.

3

Connect your bank account

Go to Settings → Bank accounts and add where you want payouts to land.

Already have Stripe? Get your API keys

1

Open the Stripe Dashboard

Go to dashboard.stripe.com and log in.

2

Navigate to API Keys

Click Developers in the top-right corner, then select API keys from the left sidebar.

3

Copy both keys

Your Publishable key is visible immediately. Click "Reveal live key" to see your Secret key. Copy both.

pk_live_51...  ← Publishable key (safe to share)
sk_live_51...  ← Secret key (keep private)
4

Paste into ChatRate onboarding

Enter both keys in the Step 4 fields and proceed to activate your account.

⚠️
Keep your secret key private Never share your sk_live_ key publicly, paste it into chat, or commit it to code. ChatRate stores it encrypted and uses it only to charge clients for your calls.
🤖
Need help? Ask the AI assistant On Step 4 of onboarding, click "Need help finding your Stripe keys?" to open a chat assistant that will walk you through the process in real time.

Calls

Running a Call

1

Client books and saves their card

You both receive a confirmation email with the room link when a booking is made.

2

Join from your dashboard

At call time, click "Join call" next to the booking. The video room opens in your browser — no app download needed.

3

Timer starts when both are connected

If you're on per-minute pricing, a running cost ticker shows the client the accumulating total in real time.

4

End the call to charge

Click "End Call". Stripe immediately charges the client's saved card. A receipt is emailed to them automatically. If they opted into a transcript, that's included too.

💸
When do I get paid? Stripe typically deposits funds to your bank within 2 business days of a successful charge. You can track all payouts in your Stripe dashboard at dashboard.stripe.com/payouts.
