Booking

How to Book a Call

The whole process takes under 2 minutes. Here's what to expect from the moment you land on your expert's page.

1

Visit the booking page

Your expert will share a link like chatrate-app.com/book/theirname — open it in any browser on any device. No app download required.

2

Fill in your details

Enter your name, email address, and the service you'd like. You'll see the expert's rate clearly displayed at the top — either a flat price per call or a per-minute rate.

3

Optional: add a transcript

If the expert offers it, you can opt in to receive a full written transcript of the call by email after it ends. The fee is shown upfront — you won't be surprised.

4

Pick a time

Select your preferred date and time. You'll receive a confirmation email with your call link as soon as the booking is made.

5

Save your card

Enter your payment card details. Your card is saved securely — it is not charged at this point. The charge only happens after your call ends, based on the actual time used (or the flat rate).

✅
Booking confirmed After submitting, you'll see a confirmation page and receive an email with your call details and a join link. Save it — you'll need it at call time.
Payment

How Pricing Works

Experts on ChatRate can charge in one of two ways:

⏱
Per Minute
You're charged for the exact time the call lasts. A live cost ticker shows the running total during the call. Pay only for what you use.
📋
Flat Rate
One fixed price regardless of call length. You know the exact amount before booking — no surprises.

The charge is applied to your saved card immediately when the call ends. You'll receive an email receipt within a few minutes.

⚠️
Nothing is charged until after your call Saving your card at booking is just an authorization hold to confirm your card is valid. Your card is only charged once the expert clicks "End Call."
The call

Joining Your Call

Calls happen directly in your browser — no software to install, no Zoom account needed.

1

Open your call link

At the time of your booking, click the join link from your confirmation email. You can also find it on your booking confirmation page.

2

Allow camera and microphone

Your browser will ask for permission to use your camera and microphone. Click "Allow" — without it, the expert won't be able to hear or see you.

3

Wait for the expert to join

The timer only starts once both parties are connected. You won't be charged for wait time.

4

Have your call

Talk, ask questions, get advice. If you're on per-minute pricing, a live cost ticker shows the running total so you're always in control of the time.

5

Call ends → you're charged

The expert ends the call, your card is charged, and you receive a receipt by email. If you opted in to a transcript, it will be included.

💡
Best experience tips Use Chrome or Edge for the most reliable call quality. A stable Wi-Fi connection is recommended. Use headphones to avoid echo.
Common questions

FAQ

Is my payment information secure?
Yes. Your card details are handled entirely by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor trusted by millions of businesses. ChatRate never stores your full card number.
What if the expert doesn't show up?
If the expert doesn't join within a reasonable time, your card will not be charged. Contact the expert directly via the email in your confirmation, or reach out to us at drumpimp@gmail.com.
Can I get a refund?
Refund requests are handled between you and the expert. If you have a dispute, email us at drumpimp@gmail.com and we'll help mediate.
Will my call be recorded?
Calls are not recorded unless you explicitly opted in to the transcript add-on during booking. Transcripts are text-only (not video/audio recordings) and are sent only to you by email.
What devices can I use?
Any device with a modern browser — desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone. Chrome and Edge give the best experience. Safari on iPhone is also fully supported.
How is my personal data used?
Your name and email are used to confirm your booking and send your receipt. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. Read the full Privacy Policy at chatrate-app.com/privacy.
